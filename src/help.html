 A Guide to RideShare

How to request a ride
  • Enter your Current address in the "Origin Location" box and enter address you want to go in "Destination Loction" box and click "Reqest A Ride"
Can i Scheduling a ride in advance ?
  • Yes, we now includes the option to schedule a ride 15 minutes to 30 days in advance using the Scheduled Rides feature.
Contacting my driver
  • If you wish to contact your driver to coordinate a pickup location or discuss other details of the ride you requested, tap the bar that displays the driver's info at the bottom of your screen.
Cancelling an request ride
  • You can cancel a trip at any time, before or after a driver has accepted your trip request
Who can use IIT rideshare?
  • Only students who are enrolled in IIT for the semester has access to the rideshare.
